Mission Combat Go - NASA at Lockheed Martin Internship

Software lead among an electrical and mechanical lead in a team of three for NASA at Lockheed Martin to deliver an internship project to help inspire, engage, and educate students in STEM disciplines. Wrote C++ code in an Arduino IDE environment to program a working Go-Kart equipped with a defense mechanism, infared tracker, & turret. As part of our project, Mission Combat Go, I wrote C++ code on Arduino IDE to explicitly allow synchronous communication between an HC-06 wireless bluetooth module, infrared sensors, and radio frequency transmitters and receivers.
