About Me

My name is Emily Lakic and I am a freshman computer science major at Binghamton University. My recent interests include software development, computer systems analysis and mathematics. At Binghamton I am a member of HackBU, ACM, Spoon University, and the BuZoo. I love hackathons and enjoy working on new projects in an effort to help better society. Hackers vs. Harassment

HackAE Collaborative Hackathon Project - Emily Lakic & Mark Caldropoli

Hackers vs. Harassment was created in an effort to improve the overpowering societal issue of bullying by collecting data using Python and presenting it in the form of a website to illustrate the complexity of the problem. Data was collected to make pie charts on the website based on questions asked in our survey. Received the #HackHarassment award from Major League Hacking at HackAE held at Binghamton University's Innovative Technologies Complex.
